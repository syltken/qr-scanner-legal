QR Scanner and Generator Pro operates on a strict no-data-collection policy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

All scanning, generation, and processing operations occur entirely on your device using advanced on-device algorithms. Your scan data and generated content never leave your device.

Pro version features including batch scanning, QR code generation, advanced format support, and premium export options all operate locally:
